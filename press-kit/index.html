About OpenTale

OpenTale is an ambitious open-source sandbox adventure game inspired by Hytale. Developed by a passionate community of creators, artists, and developers who believe in transparent, collaborative game development.

Opentale is not a 1:1 remake of Hytale, we are being careful to avoid any direct overlap with Hytale so we can both exist at the same time. Fundamentally, OpenTale is a testament to the size and strength of the community Hytale brought together.

Key Facts

  • Genre: Open-world sandbox adventure
  • Platform: PC (Windows, macOS, Linux)
  • Development: Open-source, community-driven
  • Engine: Bevy/ Vulkan
  • Release: In development (ASAP)

Core Features

  • Voxel-based world building and modification
  • Adventure mode with quests and exploration
  • Creative mode for unlimited building
  • Comprehensive modding support
  • Community-driven development

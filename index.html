What is OpenTale?

OpenTale is an up-and-coming community-driven, open-source game inspired by the spirit of Hytale. Dive into a voxel-based sandbox world where creativity, adventure and modding possibilities are endless.

🏗️ Building

Create amazing structures with our advanced building system. From simple houses to complex castles.

🎨 Creativity

Unleash your imagination with extensive modding support, custom textures, and unlimited creative possibilities.

🌍 Deep World

Explore a vast, procedurally generated world with diverse biomes, hidden secrets, and endless adventures.

⚔️ Adventure

Embark on epic quests, fight monsters, and discover the mysteries of the OpenTale universe.

👥 Community

Join our open-source community. Contribute to development and shape the future of the game.

🔧 Open Source

Fully open-source development with transparent progress and community-driven features.
